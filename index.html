ABOUT EATENFISH

EatenFish is a collection of 1500 pixel art NFTs representing digital fish that have been consumed in the vast ocean of the blockchain.

Each fish tells a story of survival, predation, and the circle of digital life in our pixelated ecosystem.

Created with love for the pixel art community, EatenFish celebrates the nostalgia of 8-bit graphics while embracing modern blockchain technology.

THE STORY BEHIND THE PIXELS

THE DIGITAL OCEAN

In the depths of the blockchain, a thriving ecosystem of pixel fish once swam freely. Their vibrant colors and unique patterns made them the jewels of the digital sea.

But as with all ecosystems, balance must be maintained. Predators lurked in the shadows of smart contracts, waiting for their moment to strike.

THE GREAT CONSUMPTION

One fateful day, a massive wave of transactions swept through the ocean. The fish, unaware of the danger, continued their pixelated dance.

One by one, they were consumed - not by traditional predators, but by the very system that gave them life. Their data was digested into the blockchain, becoming permanent parts of the ledger.
